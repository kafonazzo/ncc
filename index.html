Explore the Amalfi Coast

Experience your vacation on the magical coastline. A jewel warmed by the sun, characterized by an unrivaled gastronomic tradition, small towns overlooking the sea, and a historical heritage of inestimabile value.

Extensive and fascinating, the Amalfitan Coast is an area between the Gulf of Naples and the Gulf of Salerno, and includes 15 wonderful towns in the province of Salerno: Amalfi, Atrani, Cava de’ Tirreni, Cetara, Conca dei Marini and its Emerald Grotto, Ravello, Maiori, Minori, Scala, Vietri sul Mare…

The beaches of the Amalfi Coast are among the most beautiful in the Tyrrhenian Sea.

The food and wine tradition of the Amalfi Coast is based on typical local products, obtained thanks to its optimal climate.

Must-try products include Amalfi lemons, local wine, and the famous colatura di alici from Cetara.
